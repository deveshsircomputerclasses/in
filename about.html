About Us

DEVESH SIR COMPUTER CLASSES is committed to providing quality computer education for all. Our mission is to build strong computer literacy and career opportunities for students in rural and urban areas.

We offer a wide range of practical and job-oriented courses including Basic Computer, Tally with GST, Typing, DTP, Programming (C, C++, Python), Web Design and more. Our trained faculty ensures each student understands the concepts clearly through hands-on training and smart classes.
